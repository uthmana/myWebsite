BüyükKabaca Vakfı


Büyükabaca Culture and Education Foundation.

Those who do not have the opportunity to study at primary, secondary and tertiary levels receive a cash grant in the form of gratuitous and other grants. They offer doctoral and research grants at home and abroad. Special investigations are carried out on the matters that are needed.

Primary, secondary and tertiary level students provide housing, educational facilities, shelter places for students, open and supervise the teaching and recreation camps, lunch houses, reading and study halls, or transfer them to the relevant public institutions. It opens museums and exhibitions. They organize foreign language courses, seminars, conferences, cultural tours of the country and abroad.
